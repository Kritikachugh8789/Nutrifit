PCOS

It is a common lifestyle disorder that happens in every one out of ten women in reproductive age and is a leading cause of infertility these days.

SIGNS- irregular/no/heavy periods, excess body and facial hair, acne, pelvic pain, difficulty in getting pregnant, patches of thick, darker and velvety skin, type 2 diabetes, obesity, sleep apnea, mood swings, endocrinal cancer and many more.

DIABETES

  • 1. Diabetes mellitus is a metabolic disorder characterized by the decreased ability or complete inability of the tissues to utilize carbohydrates, accompanied by changes in metabolism of fat, protein, water and electrolytes.
  • 2. The disorder is due to a defficiency or diminished effectiveness of insulin hormone.
  • 3. The diet plan is based on the height, weight, age, sex, physical lifestyle and the type of diabetes.

MENOPAUSE

It is used to indicate the end of the period of possible sexual reproduction in women, as indicated by the cessation of mesntrual periods. Ovaries stop producing oestrogen and this leads to oestrogen defficiency which causes short term symptoms or long term health issues. Certain modifications are needed to be done in diet which helps in reducing the severity of the symptoms.
